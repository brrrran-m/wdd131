Car Showcase Site Plan

Brandan Morris

WDD 131-02

Overview

Purpose

To provide car enthusiasts with detailed information about different car models, including specifications, images, and reviews.

Audience

Car enthusiasts, potential buyers, and anyone interested in learning more about various car models.

Dynamic elements

  • Car Details Page: Clicking on a car model will take the user to a detailed page with more information about the car.
  • Search Functionality: A search bar to filter car models by name or other attributes.
  • Dynamic Content: Use JavaScript to dynamically display car details and images.
