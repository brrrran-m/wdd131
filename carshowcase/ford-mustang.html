Ford Mustang

The Ford Mustang is a classic American muscle car that has become an icon in automotive history. Known for its powerful engines, aggressive styling, and thrilling performance, the Mustang offers a driving experience that's both exciting and unforgettable.

Specifications

  • Engine: 5.0L V8
  • Horsepower: 450 hp
  • 0-60 mph: 4.2 seconds
  • Top Speed: 155 mph
  • Price: Starting at $43,000

Reviews
